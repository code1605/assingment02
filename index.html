Info

Littlejoy Special School is a junior school that provides early childhood classes from ECD upto grade Three.It is a Zimbabwean based elementary school for kids and also has a wing for those physically and mentally retarded as derived from the name special

The world is evolving in as far as technology is concerned , and the website is a brainchild of the already established new curriculum.Created by an enthusiastic developer/ programmer Rufaro Satoro we guarantee that life will be made easier in as far as bridging the infomation gap is concerned.
